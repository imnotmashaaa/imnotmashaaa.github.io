  1. Poodle: A highly intelligent and active breed of dog.
  2. Hypoallergenic: Poodles are known for their hypoallergenic coat, making them suitable for many allergy sufferers.
  3. Versatile: They are versatile and excel in various dog sports and activities.
  4. Grooming: Poodles require regular grooming due to their curly, non-shedding coat.
  5. Intelligent: They are exceptionally smart and trainable dogs.
  6. Standard: The Standard Poodle is the largest of the three Poodle varieties.
  7. Miniature: The Miniature Poodle is a smaller size variant, known for its agility and intelligence.
  8. Toy: The Toy Poodle is the tiniest among Poodle breeds, making a great companion pet.
  9. Active: Poodles are active and require regular exercise and mental stimulation.
  10. Elegant: Known for their elegant appearance and distinctive gait.
